The Best Movies According to Ashley

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kiki's Delivery Service

Fun and whimsical style of animation from Ghibli Studio.

Interview with the Vampire

Interesting take on vampires and their view and relationship toward their siring vampire.

Daybreakers

Another interesting vampire movie from the perspective of vampires in a utopia where humans are a dying breed.
